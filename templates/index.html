About Us

IT STARTED, QUITE SIMPLY, LIKE THIS...

Nanglo Restaurant is a Nepali, Indian, and Asian restaurant located in Abu Dhabi, specifically in Sabiya 11. It has been in operation since 2016, providing customers with a diverse culinary experience. We specialize in serving authentic Nepali cuisine, along with Indian and Asian dishes. As a Nepali restaurant, Nanglo offers a range of traditional Nepali food items, reflecting the rich and unique flavors of Nepal. Customers can expect to find dishes like Mo:Mo (Nepali dumplings), dal bhat (lentil soup with rice), gundruk (fermented leafy greens), sel roti (sweet rice bread), and much more. These dishes showcase the distinct Nepali spices, herbs, and cooking techniques, providing an authentic taste of Nepali cuisine.
